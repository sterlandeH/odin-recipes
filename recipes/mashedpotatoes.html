Mashed Potatoes

Description

This bacon-cheddar mashed potatoes dish is an easy and flavour dish perfect for the holidays.

Ingredients

  • Yellow-fleshed potatoes, peeled and chopped into 1-inch pieces(3 lb)
  • Unsalted Butter(6 tbsp)
  • Better Than BouillonÂ® Roasted Chicken Base(2 tbsp)
  • 2% milk(3/4 cup)
  • Shredded Cheddar cheese(1 cup)
  • Crumbled ready-cooked bacon(3/4 cup)
  • Finely chopped fresh chives(3 tbsp)

Steps

  1. In large saucepan of boiling salted water, cook potatoes for 15 to 20 minutes or until fork-tender. Drain well; return to saucepan set over low heat.
  2. Meanwhile, in saucepan set over medium heat, cook butter and chicken base for about 2 minutes or until butter is melted and starting to sizzle. Stir in milk; cook, stirring, for 2 to 3 minutes or until milk is heated through.
  3. Stir milk mixture into potatoes; mash until smooth. Fold in cheese, bacon and chives.
